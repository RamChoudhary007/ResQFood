Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Food Donate?
Food Donate is a platform to reduce food waste by connecting donors with those in need.
How can I donate food?
Click on "Donate Food" and fill out the form.
How to search for donations?
Use the search section with location and category.
Who can use this platform?
Anyone can donate or search for food donations.

About usThe basic concept of this project "Food Waste Management" is to collect the excess/leftover food from donors such as hotels, restaurants, marriage halls , etc. and distribute to the needy people .
